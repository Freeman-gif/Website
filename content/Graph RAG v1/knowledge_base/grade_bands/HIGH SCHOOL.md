# HIGH SCHOOL

## Description

High school refers to educational institutions designed for students typically aged 14 to 18 years. This stage of education comprises grades 9 through 12, making it a critical phase in the academic journey of students as they prepare for higher education or entry into the workforce. High schools must adhere to established educational standards to ensure a consistent and quality learning environment. 

In addition to providing a general education, high schools often focus on specific academic and vocational subjects, as well as social and emotional development. The curriculum is structured to cover various subjects, including mathematics, science, literature, and social studies, which are essential for comprehensive knowledge acquisition. Furthermore, high schools are equipped with test banks designed to deliver assessments that measure students' understanding and proficiency in core subjects. 

Overall, high school serves as a foundational educational level that plays a vital role in shaping students' futures, equipping them with the necessary skills and knowledge to pursue further education or career opportunities.
