# GRADE 11

## Description

Grade 11 is a significant educational level within the K-12 schooling system, highlighted for its importance in standardized assessments. It is one of the three targeted grade levels for federally mandated testing, ensuring that students meet specific educational benchmarks as they prepare for post-secondary education and future opportunities. Additionally, Grade 11 is a key focus in the development of performance assessments within the SNAP Project, which aims to evaluate the skills and knowledge of students effectively. This emphasis on Grade 11 underscores its role as a critical stage in students' academic careers, as they are assessed on their readiness for the challenges that lie ahead, both in higher education and in the workforce.
