# DROUGHT

## Description

Drought is a prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, affecting water supply for ecosystems and human use.

## Related Concepts

- [[HUMAN ACTIVITIES]] — Human activities, such as overuse of water resources, can exacerbate drought conditions and their impacts.
