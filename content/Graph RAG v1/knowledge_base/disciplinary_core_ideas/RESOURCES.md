# RESOURCES

## Description

Resources such as food, water, and minerals are essential for the survival of organisms in ecosystems, often leading to competition among species.

## Related Concepts

- [[ANIMALS]] — Animals depend on various resources for survival, such as food, water, and shelter, creating interactions that can influence their populations
- [[PLANTS]] — Plants require resources such as sunlight, water, and minerals to grow, which directly impacts the productivity of ecosystems
