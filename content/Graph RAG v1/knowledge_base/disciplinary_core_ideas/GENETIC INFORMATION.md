# GENETIC INFORMATION

## Description

Genetic information plays a crucial role in the biological functioning of organisms, as it is stored in DNA molecules located within cells. This information dictates the characteristics and functions of living beings through the process of gene expression. Essentially, genetic information encompasses the molecular basis of heredity, which primarily involves specific sequences of DNA. These sequences serve not only to determine individual traits and functions but also provide vital evidence of evolution among species. Thus, genetic information is fundamental to understanding both the biological aspects of life and the evolutionary connections that exist across different organisms.
