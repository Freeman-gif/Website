# PLATE TECTONICS

## Description

Plate tectonics is the unifying theory explaining the movements of Earth's lithospheric plates and their geological implications over time.

## Related Concepts

- [[TECTONIC PROCESSES]] — Tectonic processes are driven by the movements of Earth's plates under the theory of plate tectonics, influencing geological features
