# POLLUTION

## Description

Pollution encompasses harmful substances introduced into the environment, which can damage ecosystems and affect human health.

## Related Concepts

- [[GEOSPHERE]] — Pollution impacts the geosphere by contaminating soil and water resources, leading to detrimental effects on land ecosystems.
- [[BIOSPHERE]] — Pollution negatively influences the biosphere, resulting in species extinction and damage to natural habitats.
