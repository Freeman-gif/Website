# PREDATION

## Description

Predation is an ecological interaction where one organism (the predator) feeds on another organism (the prey), affecting population dynamics in ecosystems.

## Related Concepts

- [[ANIMALS]] — Predation involves animals preying on other animals, shaping the structure and dynamics of ecosystems
