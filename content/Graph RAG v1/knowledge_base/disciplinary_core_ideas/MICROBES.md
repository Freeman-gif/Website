# MICROBES

## Description

Microbes are microscopic organisms that play important roles in nutrient cycling and energy flow in ecosystems.

## Related Concepts

- [[DECOMPOSERS]] — Microbes function as decomposers, recycling nutrients from dead matter back into the ecosystem
