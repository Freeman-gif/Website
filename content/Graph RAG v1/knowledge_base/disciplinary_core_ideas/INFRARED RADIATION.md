# INFRARED RADIATION

## Description

Infrared radiation is a form of heat energy emitted from objects, which affects temperature and energy flow in the atmosphere.

## Related Concepts

- [[ATMOSPHERE]] — Infrared radiation influences the temperature of the atmosphere based on energy absorbed from the sun
