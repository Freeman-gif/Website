# SOUND WAVE

## Description

Sound waves are vibrations that can cause matter to vibrate, producing sound which requires a medium for transmission.

## Related Concepts

- [[MEDIUM]] — Sound waves require a medium for transmission, emphasizing the relationship between sound and the material through which it travels
- [[WAVES]] — Sound waves are a type of wave, characterized by their ability to cause vibrations in a medium
- [[WAVES]] — Sound waves are generated as a specific category of waves that transfer energy through a medium
- [[VIBRATION]] — Vibration of matter generates sound waves, linking physical motion with acoustic phenomena
