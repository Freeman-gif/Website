# LIGHT

## Description

Light is a form of electromagnetic radiation that can be reflected, absorbed, or transmitted by objects, allowing visibility.

## Related Concepts

- [[ELECTROMAGNETIC WAVES]] — Light is a form of electromagnetic wave, differing in wavelength and frequency
- [[WAVES]] — Waves include both sound and light, differing in their properties and the media they require
