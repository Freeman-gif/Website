# MEDIUM

## Description

A medium is a substance (solid, liquid, or gas) through which waves, such as sound, travel.

## Related Concepts

- [[SOUND WAVE]] — Sound waves require a medium for transmission, emphasizing the relationship between sound and the material through which it travels
