# ENERGY

## Description

Energy is the capacity to do work, involved in various physical phenomena such as motion, heat, and electricity

## Related Concepts

- [[MATTER]] — Matter and energy are intertwined concepts in physical systems, as the transfer of energy affects matter properties and behavior.
- [[OBJECT]] — Energy is present in objects due to their motion and the interactions between them
- [[FORCE]] — Force can transfer energy between objects during interactions
