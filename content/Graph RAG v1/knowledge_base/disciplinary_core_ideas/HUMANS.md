# HUMANS

## Description

Humans are living beings that play a significant role in the global ecosystem, relying on essential natural resources such as water and land for their survival and the development of societies. They have evolved into a species uniquely capable of taking precautions to respond to natural hazards, demonstrating adaptability and resilience in the face of environmental challenges. In addition to relying on these resources, humans also have a profound impact on the world's environments and resources through their activities. This interaction not only shapes their immediate surroundings but also influences the overall health and stability of ecosystems worldwide. As a result, the relationship between humans and the natural world is complex, marked by both dependency and influence.
