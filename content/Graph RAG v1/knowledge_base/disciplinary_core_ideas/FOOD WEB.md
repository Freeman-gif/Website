# FOOD WEB

## Description

A food web is a complex network of feeding relationships among organisms in an ecosystem, demonstrating how energy and nutrients flow.

## Related Concepts

- [[DECOMPOSERS]] — Decomposers play a vital role in the food web by returning nutrients to the soil, supporting plant growth
- [[PLANTS]] — Plants serve as primary producers in the food web, forming the basis of the energy pyramid in ecosystems
- [[ANIMALS]] — Animals act as consumers in the food web, depending on plants and other organisms for energy
