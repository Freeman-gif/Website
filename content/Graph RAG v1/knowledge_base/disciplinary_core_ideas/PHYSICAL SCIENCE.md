# PHYSICAL SCIENCE

## Description

Physical Science is a key disciplinary concept within the framework of the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) Science Assessment. It is recognized as one of the major disciplines assessed, encompassing a broad focus on essential topics such as matter and its properties, motion, forces, and energy. As a critical content area, Physical Science is integral to student assessments conducted under the NAEP, providing a structured approach to evaluate students' understanding of fundamental scientific principles related to the physical world. Through these assessments, the NAEP aims to gauge students' proficiency in Physical Science, ensuring a comprehensive evaluation of their scientific knowledge and skills.
