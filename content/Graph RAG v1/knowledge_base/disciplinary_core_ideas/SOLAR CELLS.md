# SOLAR CELLS

## Description

Solar cells are devices designed to absorb sunlight and convert it into electrical energy, thus capturing energy from the Sun.

## Related Concepts

- [[SUN]] — Solar cells capture energy from sunlight, which is produced by the Sun, to generate electricity
