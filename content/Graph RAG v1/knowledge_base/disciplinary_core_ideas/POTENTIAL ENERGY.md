# POTENTIAL ENERGY

## Description

Potential energy is the stored energy in a system based on the relative positions of objects and their interactions.

## Related Concepts

- [[KINETIC ENERGY]] — Kinetic and potential energy are two fundamental forms of energy in physics that describe the energy within a system
