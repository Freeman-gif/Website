# CARBOHYDRATES

## Description

Carbohydrates are organic compounds that serve as a major energy source for plants and animals and have roles in cellular structure.

## Related Concepts

- [[CELLS]] — Carbohydrates provide energy for cell activities and play structural roles in various cell types
