# BIODIVERSITY

## Description

Biodiversity encompasses the variety of life forms present within a particular ecosystem, and it plays a critical role in influencing human resources and ecosystem services. It is characterized by the abundance of different species that inhabit an ecosystem, which serves as a key indicator of the ecosystem's health and stability. The diversity of species not only contributes to the resilience of ecosystems but also affects the functioning of ecological processes, which are vital for sustaining life and providing resources for human use. Overall, biodiversity is essential for maintaining ecological balance, supporting human livelihoods, and ensuring the provision of services that are crucial for survival and well-being.
