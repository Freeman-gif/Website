# ELECTROMAGNETIC WAVES

## Description

Electromagnetic waves include various types such as radio waves and x-rays, produced by changes in electric and magnetic fields.

## Related Concepts

- [[LIGHT]] — Light is a form of electromagnetic wave, differing in wavelength and frequency
- [[MATERIALS]] — Materials interact with electromagnetic waves, influencing absorption and transmission
