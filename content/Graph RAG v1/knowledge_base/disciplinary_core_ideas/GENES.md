# GENES

## Description

Genes are units of heredity in DNA that contain the information necessary for the synthesis of proteins and influence traits in organisms.

## Related Concepts

- [[DNA]] — Genes are segments of DNA that provide the instructions for making proteins, influencing traits
- [[CHROMOSOMES]] — Genes are located on chromosomes, with each chromosome carrying relevant genetic information
- [[DNA]] — Genes are segments of DNA that provide instructions for producing proteins
- [[MUTATIONS]] — Mutations can cause changes to genes, impacting how traits are expressed in organisms
- [[SEXUAL REPRODUCTION]] — Sexual reproduction involves the combination of genes from two parents, leading to genetic variation
