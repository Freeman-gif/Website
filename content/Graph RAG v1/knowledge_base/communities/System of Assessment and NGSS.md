# System of Assessment and NGSS

## Summary

The community focuses on the System of Assessment which is designed to evaluate student performance based on the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). This entails a structured approach to assessing science education in California through specific assessment supports that assist teachers and schools in applying NGSS effectively.
