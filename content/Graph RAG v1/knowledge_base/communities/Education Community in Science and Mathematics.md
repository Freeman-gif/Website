# Education Community in Science and Mathematics

## Summary

This community comprises key entities in the field of education, primarily focusing on the interrelationship between science, mathematics, engineering design, language arts, and adaptive testing. These entities work collaboratively to enhance educational outcomes through comprehensive assessment frameworks, particularly the NAEP assessments that emphasize STEM learning and integrated curricula.
