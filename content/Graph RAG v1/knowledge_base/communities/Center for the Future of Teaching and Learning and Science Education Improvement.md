# Center for the Future of Teaching and Learning and Science Education Improvement

## Summary

The community revolves around the Center for the Future of Teaching and Learning at WestEd, which focuses on enhancing science education in California through research and collaborative efforts with various authors and experts in the field. Key relationships exist between the Center and numerous authors who contribute to the research aimed at reforming educational practices, particularly in elementary science education.
