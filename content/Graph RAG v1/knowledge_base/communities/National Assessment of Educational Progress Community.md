# National Assessment of Educational Progress Community

## Summary

The community revolves around key organizations that contribute to the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP), including the National Research Council, National Council on Measurement in Education, WestEd, and others. These entities collaborate to shape educational assessment and standards in the United States, particularly focusing on science education and its evaluation through various programs and frameworks.
