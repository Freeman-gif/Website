# California Department of Education and Assessment Community

## Summary

The community is centered around the California Department of Education (CDE) and its involvement with educational assessments, particularly through the SNAP Project and the Task Bank. The CDE plays a crucial role in overseeing education in California, managing key aspects of student assessment, particularly for Grade 11 and Grade 5, which are critical for evaluating educational performance. This interconnectedness highlights the importance of standard assessments developed and maintained by the CDE to ensure educational quality across the state.
