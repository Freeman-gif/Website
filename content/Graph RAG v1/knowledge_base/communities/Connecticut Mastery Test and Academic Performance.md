# Connecticut Mastery Test and Academic Performance

## Summary

The community focuses on educational assessments in Connecticut, specifically the Connecticut Mastery Test (CMT) and the Connecticut Academic Performance Test (CAPT). These assessments were integral to evaluating student performance across various grade levels until 2014. Both tests played a significant role in shaping educational policies and practices in the state, with their conclusion marking a notable shift in assessment strategies.
