# Stanford NGSS Assessment Community

## Summary

The community focuses on the Stanford NGSS Assessment Project, which is a collaborative initiative aimed at enhancing science education assessments in California. Central to this community are the Bechtel Team, the S.D. Bechtel Jr. Foundation, and various contributors involved in the development of assessment tasks aligned with the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). The relationships among these entities highlight the importance of funding, development, and evaluation processes in creating effective educational tools.
