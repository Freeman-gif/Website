# National Assessment Community: NAEP and Educational Assessment

## Summary

This community is centered around the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP), an organization that plays a crucial role in evaluating student performance across the United States. The key entities include NAEP and various educational assessments derived from it, such as the Science Assessment and state assessments. These entities are interlinked through relationships that contribute to understanding educational outcomes and accountability in the education system.
