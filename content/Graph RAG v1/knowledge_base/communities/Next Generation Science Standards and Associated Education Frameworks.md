# Next Generation Science Standards and Associated Education Frameworks

## Summary

The community encompasses the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) and its associated frameworks, which include Science and Engineering Practices, the State Assessment System, and Performance-Based Assessments. These entities collectively aim to redefine science education by integrating practical applications into learning and assessment processes.
