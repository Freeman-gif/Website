# Governing Board and NAEP Assessment Community

## Summary

The community is centered around the Governing Board that oversees the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) assessments. It involves relationships with national organizations that contribute to panelist nominations, and panelists who are selected to advise on educational frameworks and assessments. This community's focus on equitable educational assessment is crucial in shaping educational standards across various demographics.
