# ESOL Teachers and English Language Learners Community

## Summary

The community is centered around ESOL teachers, English Language Learners (ELLs), and various educational strategies aimed at improving ELLs' proficiency in English and their academic performance. The interrelationships among these entities highlight the collective effort to create effective learning environments for ELLs, particularly in urban and rural school contexts.
