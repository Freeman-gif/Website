# Community of Educational Assessments and Performance Evaluation

## Summary

The community focuses on the interconnections between various educational assessments, performance expectations, and the involvement of stakeholders like parents and technology in the evaluation process. These entities work collaboratively to enhance educational outcomes, particularly in science education, through standardized assessments, classroom-based tasks, and accountability measures.
