# Vermont and Wyoming Educational Assessment Community

## Summary

The community comprises educational initiatives in Vermont and Wyoming, focusing on innovative approaches to performance assessments. These states are linked by their commitment to enhancing educational outcomes through unique programs like Vermont's Portfolio Assessment and Wyoming's Body of Evidence initiative, alongside their relationship with the overarching Common Core State Standards (CCSS).
