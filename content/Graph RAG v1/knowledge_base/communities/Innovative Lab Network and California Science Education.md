# Innovative Lab Network and California Science Education

## Summary

The community is centered around the Innovative Lab Network (ILN), a collaborative consortium of 18 states working towards advancing science education through improved assessment practices and resource sharing. This network is significantly influenced by California's commitment to the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) and the involvement of various educational organizations like SCALE and the Smarter Balanced Consortium, all working together to foster a robust educational environment.
