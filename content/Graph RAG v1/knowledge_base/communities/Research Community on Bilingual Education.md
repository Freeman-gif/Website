# Research Community on Bilingual Education

## Summary

The community centers around the Bilingual Research Journal, which publishes significant research aimed at enhancing educational practices for English language learners (ELLs). Key figures such as L. Garrison, M. Klentschy, G. Gay, C. Goldenberg, and N. González are influential contributors whose work informs ESOL teachers, creating a network focused on improving educational outcomes for ELLs.
