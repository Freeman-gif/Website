# Next Generation Science Standards in California

## Summary

The community centers on the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) adopted in California, aiming to enhance educational benchmarks for science education. The NGSS is the key entity, closely linked to the California education system, which has officially adopted these standards to foster improvements in science teaching and learning.
