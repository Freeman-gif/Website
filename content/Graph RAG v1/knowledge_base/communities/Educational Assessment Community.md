# Educational Assessment Community

## Summary

The Educational Assessment Community comprises key organizations dedicated to advancing standards in educational measurement and research. Notable entities include the National Council on Measurement in Education, the American Educational Research Association, and the International Test Commission, which collaborate to promote high-quality assessment practices and educational outcomes. Their intertwined relationships underscore a collective commitment towards improving educational policies and the efficacy of measurement standards.
