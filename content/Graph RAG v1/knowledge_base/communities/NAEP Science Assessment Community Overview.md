# NAEP Science Assessment Community Overview

## Summary

The community revolves around the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) Science Assessment and associated standards, which evaluate students' scientific competencies across various domains such as Physical Science, Life Science, and Earth and Space Sciences. Key entities include the NAEP, the assessment standards, and various scientific domains, creating a comprehensive framework for assessing science education in the U.S.
