# Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) Community

## Summary

The NGSS community is focused on enhancing K-12 science education in the United States through a framework that integrates scientific practices, crosscutting concepts, and core disciplinary ideas. Key entities include the NGSS itself, performance tasks, state assessment systems, and policymakers, all of which play critical roles in improving educational methodologies and standards.
