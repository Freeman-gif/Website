# Grade 4 Scientific Investigation Standards

## Summary

The community focuses on educational standards related to scientific investigation for Grade 4 students, emphasizing controlled experimentation, the importance of defining parameters, reflective inquiry, and considering impacts on various stakeholders. The entities are interconnected through their emphasis on various aspects of scientific investigation, forming a comprehensive approach to education in this field.
