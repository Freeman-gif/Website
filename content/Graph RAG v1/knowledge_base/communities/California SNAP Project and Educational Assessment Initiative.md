# California SNAP Project and Educational Assessment Initiative

## Summary

The community centers around the California SNAP Project, aimed at enhancing science assessment systems in education through alignment with the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). Key entities include the California Department of Education and various components of the assessment framework, which all contribute to improving educational quality and student outcomes. The relationships among these entities highlight a collaborative effort to standardize and innovate educational assessments in California.
