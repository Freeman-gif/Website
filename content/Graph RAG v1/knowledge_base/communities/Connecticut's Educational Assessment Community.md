# Connecticut's Educational Assessment Community

## Summary

The community focuses on the educational assessment framework in Connecticut, highlighting the Connecticut Mastery Test (CMT), the Connecticut Academic Performance Test (CAPT), and various initiatives including the SBAC. These entities are interconnected through their common purpose of evaluating student performance and ensuring educational standards in the state.
