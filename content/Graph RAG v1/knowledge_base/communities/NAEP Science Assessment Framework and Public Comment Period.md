# NAEP Science Assessment Framework and Public Comment Period

## Summary

The community focuses on the NAEP Science Assessment Framework, which evaluates student comprehension in science, and includes elements such as the public comment period where stakeholders can provide feedback. The entities are interconnected, with the Governing Board considering the feedback submitted during this period as part of their decision-making process.
