# American Association for the Advancement of Science and Project 2061

## Summary

The community is centered around the American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS), which is involved in promoting science education and research. Significant initiatives under its umbrella include Project 2061, which aims to enhance science literacy through its publications, such as the Benchmarks for Science Literacy. This community is interconnected through their initiatives and support for standards in science education.
