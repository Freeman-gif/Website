# NAEP and Educational Assessment Network

## Summary

The community centers around the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) and various associated entities aimed at assessing student performance and educational standards across the United States. Key entities include the NAEP itself, the Science Assessment it conducts, state assessments, and multiple organizations including the American Psychological Association (APA) and the National Research Council (NRC). These entities have intricate relationships, collaborating to continuously enhance educational measurement and accountability standards within the U.S. educational framework.
