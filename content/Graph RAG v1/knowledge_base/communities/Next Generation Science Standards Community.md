# Next Generation Science Standards Community

## Summary

The community revolves around the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) and their associated frameworks, practices, and assessment systems aimed at improving K-12 science education in the United States. The entities within this community work collaboratively to enhance science education through a comprehensive approach that integrates scientific practices, crosscutting concepts, and performance-based assessments.
