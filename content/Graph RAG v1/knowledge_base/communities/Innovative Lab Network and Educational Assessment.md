# Innovative Lab Network and Educational Assessment

## Summary

The Innovative Lab Network (ILN) is a consortium of 18 states focused on enhancing educational assessments and science education. The network includes key entities such as the Council of Chief State School Officers (CCSSO) and the Stanford Center for Assessment, Learning, and Equity (SCALE), which collaborate to develop comprehensive resource banks and assessment methodologies that improve educational practices across the United States.
