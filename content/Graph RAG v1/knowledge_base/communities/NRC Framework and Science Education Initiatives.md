# NRC Framework and Science Education Initiatives

## Summary

The community centers around the NRC Framework for K–12 Science Education and its critical relationship with several organizations and individuals focused on enhancing science education in California. Key entities include the Center for the Future of Teaching and Learning at WestEd and the American Association for the Advancement of Science, all of which collaborate to improve teaching practices and academic standards in science education.
