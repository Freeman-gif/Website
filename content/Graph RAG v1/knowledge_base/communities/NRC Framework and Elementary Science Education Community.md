# NRC Framework and Elementary Science Education Community

## Summary

The community is centered around the NRC Framework for K–12 Science Education, which underpins elementary science education initiatives and practices. The relationships among the NRC Framework, Elementary Science Education, and the NAEP Science Crosscutting Concepts highlight a structured approach to science education across various grade levels, emphasizing coherence and practical application of scientific knowledge.
