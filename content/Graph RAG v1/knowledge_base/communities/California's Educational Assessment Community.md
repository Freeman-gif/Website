# California's Educational Assessment Community

## Summary

The community revolves around various educational entities in California, including the Smarter Balanced Consortium, high schools, and the implementation of the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). These entities are interconnected, focusing on enhancing student performance, particularly in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields through established assessment systems and standards.
