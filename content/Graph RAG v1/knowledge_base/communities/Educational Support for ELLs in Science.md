# Educational Support for ELLs in Science

## Summary

This community focuses on the relationship between key science vocabulary, science content, and cultural experiences to enhance the educational experience of English Language Learners (ELLs). The interconnectedness of these entities highlights the importance of language acquisition and cultural integration in science education for ELLs.
