# California Education Reform Community

## Summary

The California Education Reform Community is represented by key entities including the Smarter Balanced Consortium, California's educational system, high school structures, and the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). These entities are interconnected, focusing on enhancing science education and student performance through standardized assessments and curricular reforms. The community is characterized by its collective efforts towards educational improvement, stakeholder engagement, and a commitment to STEM disciplines.
