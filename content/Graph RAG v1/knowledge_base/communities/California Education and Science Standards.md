# California Education and Science Standards

## Summary

The community focuses on California's educational landscape, emphasizing its commitment to advancing science and engineering through the implementation of the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) and various assessment initiatives. The key entities, including California's education system, the NGSS, and various educational bodies such as high schools and the Smarter Balanced Consortium, work collaboratively to enhance educational outcomes, particularly in STEM areas.
