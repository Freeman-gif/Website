# SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING PRACTICES

## Description

Science and Engineering Practices consist of the skills and approaches used by scientists and engineers to develop explanations and solutions in science

## Related Concepts

- [[NGSS]] — The Science and Engineering Practices are a core component of the NGSS framework aimed at improving students' scientific reasoning abilities
