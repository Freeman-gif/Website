# ASSESSMENTS

## Description

Assessments are essential processes designed to evaluate and measure student performance, educational quality, and understanding of core concepts across various subjects. They serve multiple purposes, including communicating the intent of educational standards and providing insights into student comprehension.

In the context of education, assessments encompass a variety of methods and systems that aim to inform teaching practices and improve educational outcomes. They not only focus on what students know but also on how well they can apply their knowledge in real-world situations. This application of high-level thinking is particularly reflected in assessments related to science education, which align with the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). Here, assessments are utilized to measure students' understanding of core scientific principles and their ability to engage in scientific practices, thus highlighting the importance of practical application in learning.

Overall, assessments act as a crucial component in the educational framework, functioning both as measurement tools for student achievement and as feedback mechanisms for educators. By effectively utilizing assessments, educational institutions can better understand how to enhance student learning and success.
