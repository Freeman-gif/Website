# NEXT GENERATION SCIENCE ASSESSMENT PROJECT

## Description

The "Next Generation Science Assessment Project" is an initiative designed to create assessments that are aligned with the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). The project aims to modernize and enhance the way science is assessed for students, ensuring that evaluations reflect contemporary educational goals and frameworks. By focusing on innovative assessment methods, the project seeks to promote a deeper understanding of scientific concepts and practices in students. The overarching goal is to improve the quality of science assessments, making them more relevant and effective in measuring student knowledge and skills in accordance with NGSS guidelines.
