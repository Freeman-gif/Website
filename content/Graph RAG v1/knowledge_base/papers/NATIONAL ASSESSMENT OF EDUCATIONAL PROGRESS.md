# NATIONAL ASSESSMENT OF EDUCATIONAL PROGRESS

## Description

The National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) is a nationwide assessment in the United States that measures student knowledge and skills in various subjects, including science.

## Related Concepts

- [[US DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION]] — The US Department of Education oversees the NAEP, which evaluates the academic performance of students, including ELLs in science.
