# NEXT GENERATION SCIENCE STANDARDS

## Description

The Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) are a comprehensive set of educational standards that govern the teaching and assessment of science education across the United States. These standards are designed for K-12 education and aim to better prepare students for future academic pursuits and careers in the fields of science and technology. 

The NGSS were specifically implemented in California, reflecting a broader movement towards modernizing science education to ensure it meets both current and future demands. The standards emphasize not only the acquisition of scientific knowledge but also the development of critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills. 

By focusing on real-world applications and integrating cross-disciplinary approaches, the NGSS seek to engage students more deeply in the learning process, ultimately fostering a generation of learners equipped to navigate and contribute to the complexities of modern science and technology industries. The initiative represents a significant shift in educational standards, aiming for a cohesive and rigorous framework that enhances the overall quality of science education in the U.S.
