# WESTED

## Description

WestEd is a prominent research, development, and service agency dedicated to enhancing educational outcomes across various settings. The organization plays a vital role in the field of education by providing technical assistance aimed at the development and review of educational assessments. Among its key projects, WestEd is involved with the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) Science Assessment Framework, which is crucial for assessing student performance in science across the United States.

As part of its commitment to improving educational assessment practices, WestEd has been contracted to facilitate the comprehensive process of updating the NAEP Science Assessment Framework. This involvement underscores WestEd's expertise and leadership in educational research and assessment, ensuring that the framework reflects current academic standards and effectively evaluates students' understanding and knowledge in the science domain.

Overall, WestEd's initiatives and services are pivotal in shaping educational assessments, contributing to advancements in how student performance is evaluated, and ultimately supporting improved educational practices and policies nationwide.
