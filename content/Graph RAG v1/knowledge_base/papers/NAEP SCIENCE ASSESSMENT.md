# NAEP SCIENCE ASSESSMENT

## Description

The NAEP Science Assessment is a comprehensive standardized evaluation designed to assess U.S. students' engagement with scientific and engineering concepts. It focuses on multiple dimensions of student participation in science, highlighting not only their knowledge but also their reasoning skills. 

The assessment is grounded in updated frameworks and standards, ensuring that it remains relevant and reflective of current educational goals in science. Through this evaluation, insights into the scientific knowledge and skills of students are collected and reported across various categories, allowing for an in-depth understanding of students' capabilities in science and engineering.

By providing valuable data on student performance, the NAEP Science Assessment serves as a critical tool for educators and policymakers, guiding improvements in science education across the nation.
