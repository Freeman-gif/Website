# MULTIDIMENSIONAL ITEMS

## Description

MULTIDIMENSIONAL ITEMS refer to assessment tasks designed to evaluate student understanding through a variety of integrated dimensions of knowledge and skills. Specifically utilized within NAEP assessments, these items aim to provide a comprehensive evaluation of students' knowledge across different dimensions. By measuring various aspects of understanding, MULTIDIMENSIONAL ITEMS offer a nuanced approach to assessment, allowing for a deeper insight into student learning and capabilities. Through this methodology, educators and assessment creators can gauge not only the breadth of students' knowledge but also the intricacies of their skills, leading to a more holistic understanding of educational outcomes.
