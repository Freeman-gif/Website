# URBAN INSTITUTE

## Description

The Urban Institute is a prominent research organization and think tank dedicated to delivering data and analysis on a wide array of urban issues. It focuses particularly on social and economic policy challenges, utilizing rigorous research methodologies to inform public dialogue and improve policy decisions. Among the key areas of study, the Urban Institute investigates educational trends and the experiences of immigrant children in schools, highlighting critical insights into the challenges faced by English Language Learners (ELL) and their demographic characteristics. Through its comprehensive analyses, the Urban Institute aims to influence policy making and contribute to a better understanding of urban dynamics and the multifaceted challenges residents face in contemporary society.
