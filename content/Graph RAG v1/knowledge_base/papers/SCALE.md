# SCALE

## Description

SCALE, which stands for the Stanford Center for Assessment, Learning, and Equity, is a research center based at Stanford University dedicated to enhancing educational practices through innovative methodologies. The organization plays a significant role in improving the quality of education by collaborating with various educational entities, including the Council of Chief State School Officers (CCSSO), to develop educational tasks for the Innovative Lab Network. 

In its commitment to research and evaluation, SCALE has conducted comprehensive retrospective studies focused on performance assessment initiatives and systems across the United States. These research efforts aim to analyze and understand the effectiveness of various performance assessment initiatives, contributing valuable insights to the field of educational evaluation. In addition to conducting these studies, SCALE also offers formative assessments that support educational performance evaluations and assist in gauging student learning. 

Through its multifaceted approach, SCALE endeavors to advance educational practices, advocate for equity in learning, and provide resources that are essential for educators and stakeholders in the educational landscape.
