# 2028 NAEP SCIENCE ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK

## Description

The "2028 NAEP Science Assessment Framework" is an upcoming assessment structure designed to shape the administration of the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) Science Assessment in 2028. This framework will serve as a comprehensive guideline aimed at evaluating science knowledge on a national scale. Its primary objective is to establish clear benchmarks and standards for the assessment process, ensuring that it effectively measures students' understanding and competencies in science.

The framework is being developed to reflect current scientific knowledge, educational practices, and the evolving landscape of science education. By doing so, it aims to provide a robust foundation for assessing student performance and learning outcomes in science, contributing to a broader understanding of educational effectiveness across the nation.

As the implementation date approaches, the specifics of the framework will help educators and policymakers align their teaching strategies and assessment practices with the goals of the NAEP Science Assessment. The 2028 NAEP Science Assessment Framework is crucial for enhancing the national assessment ecosystem, ensuring that the measures of science knowledge are relevant, rigorous, and reflective of 21st-century science education needs.
