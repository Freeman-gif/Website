# PERFORMANCE ASSESSMENTS

## Description

Performance assessments are specialized evaluations aimed at measuring student learning and understanding across various educational contexts. They are designed to provide insightful feedback on both student performance and the effectiveness of instructional strategies. These assessments often involve students engaging in tasks, projects, or creating portfolios that demonstrate their grasp of the material.

Particularly supported by initiatives like the SNAP Project, performance assessments span all grade levels and are tailored to gauge students' capabilities in diverse subjects and skills. This approach allows educators to obtain a comprehensive view of student understanding, moving beyond traditional testing methods to include more meaningful applications of knowledge. 

Through performance assessments, educators can evaluate not only the cognitive abilities of students but also their practical application of learned concepts in real-world scenarios. This blend of assessment methodologies enables a more holistic evaluation of student performance, thereby fostering an educational environment that prioritizes detailed feedback and continuous growth.

In summary, performance assessments serve as a crucial tool for educators, providing a framework to assess student understanding effectively while also facilitating improvements in teaching practices.
