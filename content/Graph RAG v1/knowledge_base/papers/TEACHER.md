# TEACHER

## Description

A teacher is a professional whose primary role is to educate students and assess their performance. This multifaceted responsibility encompasses not only the delivery of instruction but also the careful evaluation of student progress and understanding. Teachers are tasked with employing various assessment strategies to gather data on student learning, which is then utilized to inform and refine their instructional practices. 

In order to effectively fulfill these duties, teachers must implement assessments tailored to their curriculum and student needs. The results of these assessments serve as critical feedback, guiding teachers in adapting their teaching methodologies to better support student learning outcomes. 

Additionally, there exists a trained group of teachers who are specifically responsible for scoring the performance tasks assigned within the assessment system. This collaborative approach helps ensure consistency and fairness in evaluating student performance across different educational settings.

Overall, teachers play a vital role in the educational ecosystem, facilitating learning and growth while continuously adapting to the diverse needs of their students through data-informed practices.
