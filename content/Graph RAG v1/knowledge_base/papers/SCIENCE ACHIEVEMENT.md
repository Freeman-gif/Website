# SCIENCE ACHIEVEMENT

## Description

Science achievement refers to the performance of students in science subjects, particularly as measured by standardized tests like the NAEP.

## Related Concepts

- [[ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK]] — The NAEP Science Assessment Framework is developed to effectively evaluate science achievement among students
- [[4TH GRADERS]] — 4th graders are included in the NAEP assessments that measure science achievement and performance standards among young students.
- [[8TH GRADERS]] — 8th graders are also evaluated in NAEP assessments on their science achievement, showing trends across grades over time.
- [[12TH GRADERS]] — 12th graders are assessed for their science achievement in the NAEP, highlighting performance challenges faced by ELLs.
- [[URBAN INSTITUTE]] — The Urban Institute's studies provide insight into science achievement gaps, particularly for ELLs when compared to their English-speaking peers.
