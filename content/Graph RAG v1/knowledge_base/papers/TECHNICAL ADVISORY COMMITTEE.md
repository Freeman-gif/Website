# TECHNICAL ADVISORY COMMITTEE

## Description

The Technical Advisory Committee is a specialized group comprising measurement experts who offer critical feedback concerning technical issues related to the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) Science assessment. This committee plays a significant role in ensuring the robustness and accuracy of the assessment framework by addressing various measurement challenges and providing expert insights. Their collective expertise is crucial in refining the methodologies employed in the assessment, thereby enhancing the overall quality and reliability of the information derived from the NAEP Science assessments. Through their evaluations and recommendations, the Technical Advisory Committee contributes to the development of a more effective framework that supports educational measurement and assessment practices.
