# 2028 NAEP SCIENCE FRAMEWORK

## Description

The 2028 NAEP Science Framework is an upcoming assessment framework designed to guide the development of National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) science assessments. This framework is intended to enhance the evaluation and reporting processes associated with science education content. It incorporates various updates and changes aimed at improving the overall assessment of student performance in science. As such, the framework seeks to provide a comprehensive approach to evaluating scientific knowledge and skills among students, thereby ensuring that assessments are relevant and effective in measuring educational outcomes in the field of science.
