# NATIONAL CENTER FOR EDUCATION STATISTICS

## Description

The National Center for Education Statistics (NCES) is a key federal agency under the U.S. Department of Education dedicated to the collection, analysis, and reporting of education-related data within the United States. It serves as the primary entity for gathering comprehensive information on various aspects of the U.S. education system. NCES conducts numerous studies and assessments, including the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP), which provides insight into educational achievements nationwide.

In addition to general educational statistics, NCES focuses on specific populations, including English learners, ensuring that data reflects the diverse experiences and academic outcomes of all students. With its robust methodology and extensive datasets, NCES plays a vital role in informing policy decisions, educational practices, and research initiatives aimed at improving educational outcomes across the country. Overall, NCES stands as a crucial source of reliable and comprehensive data essential for understanding and enhancing education in the United States.
