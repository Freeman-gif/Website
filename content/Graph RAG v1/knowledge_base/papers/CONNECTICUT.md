# CONNECTICUT

## Description

Connecticut is a U.S. state located in the northeastern region of the country, notable for its commitment to educational assessment and reform. The state has administered the Connecticut Mastery Test (CMT) since 1985 and the Connecticut Academic Performance Test (CAPT) since 1994. These assessments are integral to evaluating student performance within the education system, reflecting Connecticut's focus on academic standards and educational quality.

Over the years, Connecticut has been recognized for its strong leadership in education, which has fostered the implementation and evolution of various assessment systems. A key aspect of its educational reforms has been the introduction of performance-based assessment formats in the 1990s, allowing for a more comprehensive evaluation of student learning and capabilities.

The state has demonstrated a commitment to continuously improving its assessment strategies, which have persisted for over 25 years. This longevity indicates not only the effectiveness of these tests in tracking student progress but also the state's proactive stance in educational leadership. As a result, Connecticut stands out among U.S. states for its innovative approach to educational assessments and the emphasis on ensuring that students meet rigorous performance standards.
