# PISA

## Description

PISA, which stands for the Programme for International Student Assessment, is an international evaluation designed to assess the educational performance of 15-year-old students in reading, mathematics, and science literacy. This assessment provides valuable insights into how students from various countries perform in these critical areas of knowledge and skills. PISA has become a key tool for educational policy makers and researchers to analyze the effectiveness of educational systems worldwide.

Recently, PISA has begun transitioning towards computer-based testing, reflecting advancements in technology and the need to evaluate digital literacy alongside traditional skills. This move aims to modernize the assessment process and align it more closely with the skills that are increasingly relevant in today’s digital age. As such, PISA continues to evolve, ensuring that it remains a relevant and comprehensive evaluation of student competencies on a global scale. Overall, PISA plays a crucial role in understanding and improving educational outcomes across the globe.
