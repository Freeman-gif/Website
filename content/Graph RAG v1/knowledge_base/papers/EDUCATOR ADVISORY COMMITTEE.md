# EDUCATOR ADVISORY COMMITTEE

## Description

The Educator Advisory Committee is a specialized group comprised of science educators who play a crucial role in providing feedback on matters pertinent to classroom practitioners. This committee focuses specifically on issues related to the National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) Science assessment. By leveraging the insights and experiences of its members, the Educator Advisory Committee aims to ensure that the science assessment is aligned with the practical needs and realities faced by educators in the field. Their feedback is instrumental in shaping policies and practices that impact science education, helping to enhance the effectiveness and relevance of the assessments administered to students.
