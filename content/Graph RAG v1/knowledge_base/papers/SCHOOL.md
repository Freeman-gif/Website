# SCHOOL

## Description

Schools are institutions dedicated to providing education to students, creating supportive environments conducive to learning. They play a crucial role in the education system by delivering structured curricula, fostering intellectual development, and promoting social skills among students. In addition to classroom instruction, schools implement various assessment systems to evaluate students' learning progress and understanding of the material taught. These assessments are essential for identifying student strengths and weaknesses, guiding instructional practices, and ensuring that educational objectives are met. Overall, schools serve as foundational establishments in shaping the educational journey of students, preparing them for future academic challenges and personal growth.
