# NUCLEIC ACIDS

## Description

Nucleic acids, including DNA and RNA, are biomolecules that carry genetic information essential for the reproduction and functioning of all living organisms.

## Related Concepts

- [[CELLS]] — Nucleic acids are located in the cell nucleus (DNA) and are essential for the storage and transmission of genetic information
