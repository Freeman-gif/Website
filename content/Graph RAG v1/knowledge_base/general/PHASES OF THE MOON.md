# PHASES OF THE MOON

## Description

Phases of the Moon refer to the cyclic changes in the Moon's appearance from Earth, caused by its position relative to the Sun and Earth."

## Related Concepts

- [[MOON]] — The phases of the Moon are a direct consequence of its position relative to the Earth and the Sun during its orbit.
