# FOSSIL RECORD

## Description

The fossil record represents the collection of fossils and their chronological order, documenting the existence, diversity, and extinction of many life forms throughout Earth's history.

## Related Concepts

- [[NATURAL SELECTION]] — The fossil record provides evidence supporting the theory of natural selection by documenting changes and extinctions throughout evolutionary history
- [[ANATOMICAL STRUCTURES]] — Anatomical structures of organisms can be inferred from the fossil record, allowing for the reconstruction of evolutionary relationships
