# HANDS-ON ACTIVITIES

## Description

Hands-on activities are practical and interactive learning experiences designed to facilitate direct engagement with science concepts for students. These activities enable learners to actively participate in the educational process by using materials and exploring scientific principles through experimentation and exploration. By incorporating hands-on activities into the curriculum, educators enhance students' understanding of complex theories and foster a more immersive learning environment. This approach not only strengthens comprehension of scientific ideas but also encourages critical thinking and problem-solving skills as students learn through real-world applications. Overall, hands-on activities play a crucial role in enriching science education by bridging theory with practical application.
