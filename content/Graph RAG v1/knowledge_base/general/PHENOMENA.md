# PHENOMENA

## Description

PHENOMENA are observable events that play a crucial role in driving student sensemaking and serve as focal points for science assessments. These phenomena refer to specific real-world events or processes that are employed as the basis for assessment tasks. In this context, they engage students in active sensemaking and problem-solving activities.

By using tangible phenomena, educators can create assessment tasks that require students to investigate and analyze real-world situations, thereby enhancing their understanding of scientific concepts. This approach promotes an interactive learning environment, where students can apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios, fostering deeper comprehension and engagement in the subject matter.

Overall, PHENOMENA are not just mere occurrences; they are key elements that facilitate an integrative educational experience, enabling students to utilize critical thinking and scientific reasoning in their learning process.
