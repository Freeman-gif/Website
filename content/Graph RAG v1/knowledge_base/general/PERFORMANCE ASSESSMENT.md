# PERFORMANCE ASSESSMENT

## Description

Performance Assessment refers to a method employed to evaluate students' abilities and knowledge through diverse tasks and projects, moving away from traditional standardized tests. This approach was particularly significant during the 1990s when performance assessment initiatives represented large-scale educational programs in the United States. These programs were designed not only to assess student performance but also to promote accountability within the American education system. By focusing on practical applications of knowledge, performance assessments aimed to provide a more comprehensive evaluation of students’ capabilities compared to conventional testing methods. This shift reflected a broader trend in education towards fostering critical thinking and real-world problem-solving skills among students.
