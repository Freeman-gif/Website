# FOSSILS

## Description

Fossils are preserved remnants of ancient organisms found in sedimentary rocks, providing crucial evidence for understanding Earth's biological and geological history.

## Related Concepts

- [[GEOSCIENTIFIC TIME SCALE]] — Fossils help establish the relative age of rock layers and events in the geos scientific time scale, indicating periods of life on Earth
