# KEPLER'S LAWS

## Description

Kepler's laws describe the orbital motion of planets around the Sun, emphasizing their elliptical paths and the relationship between orbital periods and distances from the Sun.

## Related Concepts

- [[SOLAR SYSTEM]] — Kepler's laws explain the motion of planets within the Solar System, directly related to the gravitational forces at play.
