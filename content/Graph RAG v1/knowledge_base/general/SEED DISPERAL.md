# SEED DISPERAL

## Description

Seed dispersal is the mechanism by which seeds are spread from the parent plant to new locations for germination.

## Related Concepts

- [[PLANT REPRODUCTION]] — Seed dispersal plays a critical role in plant reproduction as it allows for the spreading of plants to new areas for growth.
