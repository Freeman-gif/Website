# CCSSO

## Description

CCSSO, or the Council of Chief State School Officers, is an organization dedicated to supporting state education leaders in their efforts to enhance the quality of education across the United States. One of its key initiatives includes the development of a curated task bank, which serves as a resource for educators to access high-quality educational materials and assessments. This task bank is designed to facilitate effective teaching and learning practices.

In addition to this, CCSSO sponsors the Innovative Lab Network, a collaborative initiative aimed at improving educational resource banks. The network focuses on fostering innovation within the education sector, providing state education leaders with tools and strategies to implement effective educational practices. Through this work, CCSSO is committed to advancing educational resources that cater to the diverse needs of students and educators alike, ultimately striving to enhance the overall landscape of education in America.
