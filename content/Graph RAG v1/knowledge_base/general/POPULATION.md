# POPULATION

## Description

A population consists of a group of individuals of the same species living in a specific area, interacting with one another and their environment.

## Related Concepts

- [[OFFSPRING]] — Within a population, offspring are produced through the reproductive processes of organisms
- [[TRAITS]] — The traits within a population can show variation based on genetic differences and environmental influences
