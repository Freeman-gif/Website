# NATIONAL COUNCIL ON MEASUREMENT IN EDUCATION

## Description

The National Council on Measurement in Education (NCME) is an organization dedicated to the field of educational measurement and assessment. NCME's primary focus is on advancing standards in assessment practices and fostering the appropriate use of educational measurement. Through its initiatives, NCME aims to enhance the quality and effectiveness of assessments within educational contexts, thereby supporting improved educational outcomes. The organization plays a significant role in promoting best practices, providing resources, and facilitating discussions among professionals in the field of measurement and assessment.
