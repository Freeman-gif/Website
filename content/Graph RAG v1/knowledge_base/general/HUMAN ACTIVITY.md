# HUMAN ACTIVITY

## Description

Human activities that influence biodiversity include overpopulation, habitat destruction, pollution, and invasive species introduction.

## Related Concepts

- [[BIODIVERSITY]] — Human activity negatively impacts biodiversity through various mechanisms such as habitat destruction and pollution
