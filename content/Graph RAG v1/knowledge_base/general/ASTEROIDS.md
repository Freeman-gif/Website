# ASTEROIDS

## Description

Asteroids are small rocky bodies that orbit the Sun, primarily located in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter, contributing to the understanding of the early Solar System."

## Related Concepts

- [[SUN]] — Asteroids orbit the Sun and are remnants from the formation of the Solar System, providing insights into its history.
