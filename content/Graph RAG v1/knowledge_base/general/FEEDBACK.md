# FEEDBACK

## Description

Feedback refers to information provided to students and teachers about their performance for the purpose of enhancing learning and teaching effectiveness

## Related Concepts

- [[STUDENTS]] — Students receive feedback to help them understand their learning progress and improve their work
- [[TEACHERS]] — Teachers use feedback from students to make informed pedagogic decisions about their teaching
- [[COMPUTERIZATION]] — Computerization in assessments enables instant feedback for both teachers and students regarding performance
