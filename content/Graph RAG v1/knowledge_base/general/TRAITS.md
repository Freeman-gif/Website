# TRAITS

## Description

Traits are distinguishing characteristics or attributes that can be inherited or influenced by environmental factors in an organism.

## Related Concepts

- [[OFFSPRING]] — Offspring inherit traits from their parents, leading to similarities and differences among siblings
- [[ORGANISM]] — Organisms develop various traits influenced by genetic and environmental factors
- [[POPULATION]] — The traits within a population can show variation based on genetic differences and environmental influences
