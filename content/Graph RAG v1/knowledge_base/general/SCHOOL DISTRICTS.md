# SCHOOL DISTRICTS

## Description

School districts are governmental entities that oversee local public schools, involved in educational decision-making and assessment processes.

## Related Concepts

- [[TEACHERS]] — Teachers work within school districts to deliver education and are involved in assessment decision-making
- [[PARENTS]] — Parents engage with school districts regarding educational policies and assessment systems
