# LANGUAGE SUPPORT STRATEGIES

## Description

Language Support Strategies are instructional methods designed to assist English Language Learners (ELLs) in achieving proficiency in English while also enhancing their understanding of academic content. These strategies specifically focus on helping ELLs navigate the complexities of English, which is crucial for their overall academic success, particularly in subjects such as science. By integrating language acquisition with content learning, these methods aim to create a supportive educational environment that caters to the unique needs of ELLs, enabling them to become more confident and competent in using English in various academic contexts.
