# TECHNICAL QUALITY ISSUES

## Description

Technical quality issues refer to the challenges and problems related to the integrity and reliability of performance assessments.

## Related Concepts

- [[STANDARDIZATION AND COMPARABILITY]] — Lack of standardization and comparability is a significant technical quality issue that impacts performance assessments.
- [[VALIDITY AND CONTENT ISSUES]] — Validity and content issues are central to the discussion of technical quality in assessments as they determine the relevance of what is being tested.
- [[SCHOOL-LEVEL ACCOUNTABILITY]] — Technical quality issues affect how accountability is measured at the school level, ensuring that assessments are fair and valid.
- [[INTER-RATER RELIABILITY]] — Inter-rater reliability is a critical technical quality issue that affects the consistency of assessment results.
