# ECLIPSE

## Description

An eclipse occurs when one celestial body moves into the shadow of another celestial body, significantly affecting the appearance of the Sun or Moon as seen from Earth."

## Related Concepts

- [[MOON]] — An eclipse often involves the Moon blocking the Sun's light from reaching Earth, showcasing the dynamics of celestial movements.
