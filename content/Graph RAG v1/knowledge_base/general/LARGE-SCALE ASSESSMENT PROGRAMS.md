# LARGE-SCALE ASSESSMENT PROGRAMS

## Description

Large-scale assessment programs are comprehensive evaluations that cover a significant population of students, typically across a state or country.

## Related Concepts

- [[SCHOOL-LEVEL ACCOUNTABILITY]] — Large-scale assessment programs are integral to maintaining school-level accountability as they evaluate student outcomes on a broad scale.
- [[PERFORMANCE ASSESSMENTS]] — Performance assessments are included in large-scale assessment programs to evaluate student performance effectively.
- [[SCALE]] — SCALE's research is aimed at improving large-scale assessment programs through insights gained from past performance initiatives.
