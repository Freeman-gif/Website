# ASSESSMENT

## Description

Assessment refers to the process of evaluating student learning and performance through various methods, including mandated evaluations, tasks, and tests. This evaluation process is essential in educational settings as it helps measure the effectiveness of teaching and the extent of student understanding. Through a combination of different assessment formats, educators can gain insights into student progress and identify areas that need improvement. Ultimately, the aim of assessment is to enhance educational outcomes by providing feedback that informs instruction and supports student development.
