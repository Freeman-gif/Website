# TESOL

## Description

Teachers of English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL) is an organization dedicated to promoting quality education and professional development for teachers of English language learners.

## Related Concepts

- [[ELL]] — TESOL creates standards and strategies aimed at improving the education of ELLs, addressing their academic needs.
- [[DIVERSITY AND EQUITY IN SCIENCE EDUCATION]] — TESOL addresses issues of diversity and equity in science education through teacher training and curriculum development initiatives for ELLs.
