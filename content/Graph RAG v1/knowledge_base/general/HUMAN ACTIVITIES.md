# HUMAN ACTIVITIES

## Description

Human activities include actions such as industrial processes, agriculture, and urbanization that can influence natural systems and increase risks of hazards.

## Related Concepts

- [[BIOSPHERE]] — Human activities have significantly altered the biosphere, leading to changes in habitats and biodiversity.
- [[ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS]] — Engineering solutions are necessary to address the negative impacts of human activities on Earth's resources and environment.
- [[SUSTAINABILITY]] — Sustainability practices focus on regulating human activities to mitigate environmental damage and ensure resource availability.
- [[DROUGHT]] — Human activities, such as overuse of water resources, can exacerbate drought conditions and their impacts.
