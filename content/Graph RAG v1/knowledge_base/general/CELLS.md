# CELLS

## Description

Cells are the fundamental structural and functional units of life, facilitating essential biological processes through chemical reactions among various molecules.

## Related Concepts

- [[WATER]] — Water is a fundamental component of cells, crucial for maintaining cell structure and facilitating biochemical reactions
- [[PROTEINS]] — Proteins are synthesized within cells and are essential for their structure and function
- [[CARBOHYDRATES]] — Carbohydrates provide energy for cell activities and play structural roles in various cell types
- [[LIPIDS]] — Lipids are integral to cell membranes, allowing for cell structure and function
- [[ORGANS]] — Cells are the building blocks that combine to form organs, each with specialized functions in the body
- [[GENETIC INFORMATION]] — Cells contain genetic information that governs their functions and characteristics
- [[NUCLEIC ACIDS]] — Nucleic acids are located in the cell nucleus (DNA) and are essential for the storage and transmission of genetic information
