# VALIDITY AND CONTENT ISSUES

## Description

Validity and content issues deal with whether the assessment measures what it claims to and accurately reflects the curriculum being taught.

## Related Concepts

- [[TECHNICAL QUALITY ISSUES]] — Validity and content issues are central to the discussion of technical quality in assessments as they determine the relevance of what is being tested.
