# ANATOMICAL STRUCTURES

## Description

Anatomical structures refer to the physical form and organization of organisms, which can show similarities and differences that help reconstruct evolutionary history.

## Related Concepts

- [[FOSSIL RECORD]] — Anatomical structures of organisms can be inferred from the fossil record, allowing for the reconstruction of evolutionary relationships
