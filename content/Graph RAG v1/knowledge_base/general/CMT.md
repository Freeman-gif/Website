# CMT

## Description

CMT, or the Connecticut Mastery Test, was a statewide assessment administered in Connecticut targeting students in grades 4, 8, and 12 across various subject areas. This assessment program was designed to evaluate students' mastery of grade-level content standards. The CMT utilized diverse performance assessment formats to gauge the understanding and skills of students effectively. Overall, the CMT played a significant role in the educational landscape of Connecticut, providing insights into student performance and helping to inform educational practices and policies.
