# CARBON CYCLE

## Description

The carbon cycle describes the process through which carbon is exchanged among the biosphere, atmosphere, oceans, and geosphere.

## Related Concepts

- [[CELLULAR RESPIRATION]] — Cellular respiration plays a key role in the carbon cycle by releasing carbon dioxide back into the atmosphere
- [[PHOTOSYNTHESIS]] — Photosynthesis is vital for the carbon cycle as it absorbs carbon dioxide and produces oxygen
