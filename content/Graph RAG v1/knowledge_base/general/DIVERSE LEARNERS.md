# DIVERSE LEARNERS

## Description

Diverse learners are students with rich cultural and linguistic backgrounds who experience the assessment differently.

## Related Concepts

- [[NAEP SCIENCE ASSESSMENT]] — The NAEP Science Assessment is designed to be responsive to the diverse learners taking it, acknowledging their backgrounds and experiences
