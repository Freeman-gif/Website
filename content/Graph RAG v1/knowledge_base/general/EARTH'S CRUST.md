# EARTH'S CRUST

## Description

The Earth's crust is the outermost layer of the Earth, composed of solid rocks and minerals, and is involved in geological processes including tectonic activity.

## Related Concepts

- [[TECTONIC PROCESSES]] — The interaction of tectonic plates at the Earth's crust leads to the formation of geological features such as mountains and valleys
