# PARENTS

## Description

Parents play a pivotal role in the educational ecosystem, acting as vital participants who significantly influence the success of educational policies and initiatives. As guardians of students, they are deeply involved in discussions and decisions that directly affect their children's education. Their engagement is crucial not only in supporting their children’s academic journey but also in monitoring their educational progress through various assessment outcomes.

Moreover, parents are recognized as key stakeholders within the education system. Their understanding of the assessments and the rationale behind them is essential for fostering a collaborative environment that benefits both students and educators. This involvement helps ensure that the educational strategies and assessments implemented align with the needs and aspirations of their children.

Through their active participation, parents not only advocate for their children's needs but also contribute to shaping an educational landscape that supports effective learning. By influencing educational decisions, parents help bridge the gap between home and school, ensuring coherent support for students' academic and emotional well-being. Thus, the engagement of parents is integral to the educational process, ultimately impacting students' success and the overall effectiveness of educational initiatives.
