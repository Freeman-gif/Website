# OFFSPRING

## Description

Offspring refers to the descendants produced by an organism through reproduction, sharing genetic traits with their parents.

## Related Concepts

- [[TRAITS]] — Offspring inherit traits from their parents, leading to similarities and differences among siblings
- [[POPULATION]] — Within a population, offspring are produced through the reproductive processes of organisms
