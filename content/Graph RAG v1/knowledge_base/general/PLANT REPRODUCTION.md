# PLANT REPRODUCTION

## Description

Plant reproduction involves various methods, including pollination and seed dispersal, often influenced by animal behavior.

## Related Concepts

- [[POLLEN TRANSFER]] — Pollen transfer is essential for the fertilization process in flowering plants, directly contributing to plant reproduction.
- [[ANIMAL BEHAVIOR]] — Animal behaviors like pollination directly impact the success of plant reproduction.
- [[SEED DISPERAL]] — Seed dispersal plays a critical role in plant reproduction as it allows for the spreading of plants to new areas for growth.
