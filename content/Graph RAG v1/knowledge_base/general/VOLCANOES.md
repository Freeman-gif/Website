# VOLCANOES

## Description

Volcanoes are geological formations where magma from beneath the Earth's crust escapes to the surface, often resulting in eruptions that can reshape landscapes.

## Related Concepts

- [[TECTONIC PROCESSES]] — Volcanoes often form as a result of tectonic processes, particularly at divergent and convergent plate boundaries
