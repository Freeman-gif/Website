# CELL DIVISION

## Description

Cell division is a process through which a parent cell divides to produce daughter cells, allowing for growth and development in multicellular organisms.

## Related Concepts

- [[PHOTOSYNTHESIS]] — Photosynthesis provides energy and raw materials necessary for cell division and growth in organisms.
