# HERITABLE TRAITS

## Description

Heritable traits are genetic characteristics that can be passed from one generation to another, influencing natural selection processes.

## Related Concepts

- [[NATURAL SELECTION]] — Heritable traits are central to the mechanism of natural selection, as they determine which traits are favored in a given environment
