# ESOL TEACHERS

## Description

A group of educators specializing in teaching English to speakers of other languages, advocating for effective practices for English language learners (ELLs).

## Related Concepts

- [[ELLS]] — ESOL teachers develop and implement specific strategies to meet the academic needs of ELLs, thus enhancing their overall educational experience.
- [[BILINGUAL RESEARCH JOURNAL]] — The Bilingual Research Journal publishes research that informs effective teaching practices for ESOL teachers, contributing to the education of ELLs.
