# ADAPTATION

## Description

Adaptation encompasses the changes in species that occur over generations in response to environmental conditions, influencing survival and reproduction.

## Related Concepts

- [[NATURAL SELECTION]] — Natural selection facilitates adaptation by favoring traits that enhance survival in response to environmental changes
- [[ECOLOGICAL CHANGES]] — Adaptation occurs as a response to ecological changes that alter survival conditions in an environment
