The "RESOURCE BANK" is a comprehensive collection designed to support educators in enhancing their assessment practices. It features high-quality performance tasks and rubrics specifically aligned with the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) and the Common Core State Standards (CCSS). This curated resource serves as an invaluable tool for teachers aiming to implement effective assessment strategies in their classrooms.

The RESOURCE BANK includes a variety of tasks and resources that are tailored to improve educational assessments, ensuring that educators have access to reliable and relevant materials. By aligning with both NGSS and CCSS, the RESOURCE BANK fosters a holistic approach to classroom assessments, enabling teachers to evaluate student performance accurately and effectively.

In summary, the RESOURCE BANK is a vital asset for teachers seeking to enhance their instructional practices through well-developed and standards-aligned performance tasks and rubrics.
