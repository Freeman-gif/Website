# S4.21

## Description

S4.21 requires students to plan and carry out fair tests where variables are controlled to improve models or prototypes.

## Related Concepts

- [[GRADE 4]] — S4.21 is an educational standard for Grade 4 that emphasizes the importance of controlled experimentation
- [[S4.22]] — S4.22 complements S4.21 by focusing on the parameters that need to be defined for successful testing
- [[S4.23]] — S4.23 encourages reflection and inquiry, which is part of the investigative process outlined in S4.21
- [[S4.24]] — S4.24's consideration of impacts aligns with the planning aspect of investigation highlighted in S4.21
