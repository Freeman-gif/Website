# SCORING RUBRIC

## Description

The "SCORING RUBRIC" is a structured tool designed to evaluate student work products through a clear and consistent set of criteria. This rubric enhances the assessment process by providing well-defined standards, which helps educators maintain uniformity in grading various performance tasks. By outlining specific expectations and benchmarks, a scoring rubric not only facilitates objective evaluation but also aids students in understanding the important aspects of their work that will be assessed. Overall, the scoring rubric serves as a vital instrument for educators to effectively communicate performance expectations and assess student learning outcomes.
