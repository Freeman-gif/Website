# SOLAR SYSTEM

## Description

The Solar System consists of the Sun and a collection of objects, including planets, moons, and asteroids, held in orbit by the Sun's gravitational pull.

## Related Concepts

- [[SUN]] — The Sun is the central star of the Solar System, providing the gravitational force that governs the orbits of all celestial objects within it.
- [[KEPLER'S LAWS]] — Kepler's laws explain the motion of planets within the Solar System, directly related to the gravitational forces at play.
