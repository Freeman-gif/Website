# DISTRIBUTED SCORING

## Description

Distributed scoring is an approach to scoring that involves blind scoring of randomly assigned student responses by a cadre of trained scorers to improve assessment reliability.

## Related Concepts

- [[LOCAL SCORING]] — Distributed scoring is proposed as a means to improve the reliability of assessments compared to local scoring methods
- [[PERFORMANCE TASKS]] — Distributed scoring can be particularly effective for scoring performance tasks to ensure reliability
