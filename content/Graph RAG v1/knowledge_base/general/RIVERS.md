# RIVERS

## Description

Rivers are natural flowing watercourses that shape the landscape by eroding rocks and transporting sediments to oceans, lakes, and other water bodies.

## Related Concepts

- [[OCEANS]] — Rivers transport sediments and water to oceans, influencing both the landscape and geological processes
