# NGSS DIMENSIONS

## Description

NGSS dimensions refer to the three dimensions outlined in the Next Generation Science Standards which guide the development of science tasks.

## Related Concepts

- [[TEACHERS]] — Teachers design tasks based on NGSS dimensions to assess student understanding and performance in science.
- [[STUDENTS]] — Students are assessed on their understanding of the NGSS dimensions through tasks designed by teachers.
