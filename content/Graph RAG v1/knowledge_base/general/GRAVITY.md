# GRAVITY

## Description

Gravity is a fundamental force that governs the motion of celestial bodies, affecting orbits and interactions between different objects in space.

## Related Concepts

- [[SUN]] — Gravity is the force exerted by the Sun that keeps the planets, including Earth, in orbit around it.
- [[NEWTON'S LAWS]] — Newton's laws describe the gravitational forces and motions of celestial bodies, including how gravity affects their orbits.
