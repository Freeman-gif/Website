# Students t-distrbution


---
### Definition 

Let z follow a N(0,1) and $\chi^2(v)$ follow a chi-square distribution with v [[degrees of freedom]] then:
$$t= \frac{z}{\sqrt{\frac{\chi^2(v)}{v}  }}$$
follows a t distribution with v degrees of freedom
